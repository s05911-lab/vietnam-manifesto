Our Plan for Change: Four Pillars

01
SKILLS FIRST, NOT DEGREES FIRST

We transform vocational education into the pride of Vietnam.

  • National Apprenticeship System with 150% tax credits
  • 20 Vocational Excellence Centers by 2030
  • 30% wage premium for certified skilled trades
  • Partnership with German, Japanese & Korean institutions

Investment: 45 trillion VND over 5 years

02
LIFELONG LEARNING FOR EVERY WORKER

We end the idea that education stops at 22.

  • 10 million VND annual Skills Savings Account per worker
  • Mandatory company reskilling for automation
  • 40% of courses offered evenings/weekends
  • Double credits for single mothers & rural workers

Investment: 80 trillion VND annually

03
ATTRACT TOMORROW'S INDUSTRIES

We produce workers with advanced skills to attract high-tech companies.

  • Industry-Education Partnerships with global firms
  • English + Technical Skills for all vocational students
  • 10,000 annual STEM scholarships for rural students
  • 60% of scholarships for women & ethnic minorities

Investment: 35 trillion VND over 5 years

04
RECOGNIZE SKILLS, NOT JUST CERTIFICATES

We value real skills over paper credentials.

  • National Skills Testing Centers in all 63 provinces
  • Micro-credentials for specific skills
  • Prior Learning Recognition for work experience
  • Practical competency tests, no classroom required

Investment: 15 trillion VND over 4 years

The Equity Promise

This plan will fail if it only helps the privileged. We commit to comprehensive equity across all dimensions:

Geographic Equity

50% of all vocational excellence centers located outside Hanoi, HCMC, and Da Nang. High-quality education comes to rural Vietnam.

Gender Equity

Equal pay for equal skills mandated. Active recruitment of women into high-paying technical fields through targeted scholarships and harassment-free workplace guarantees.

Generational Equity

Young people get opportunities, but so do current workers facing automation. No generation is sacrificed for another.

Disability Inclusion

Adaptive technology and modified training programs. Target: 5% of all apprenticeships reserved for workers with disabilities.
